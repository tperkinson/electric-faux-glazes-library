Official Mayco page for SW-118 Sea Salt.

Key details exposed on the current product page:

  • cone 6 result is a white matte glaze with a sage green crystal
  • as the crystal melts, green blooms appear and produce a foamy textured surface
  • cone 10 shifts the base toward pale lavender-gray with mint-green crystals
  • Mayco suggests SW-106 Alabaster under Sea Salt when a lighter crystal effect is wanted

The page also marks the glaze dinnerware safe and gives the fuller crystal-glaze handling note about aggressive mixing and redistributing settled crystals.
