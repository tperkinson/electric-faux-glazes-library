Official Mayco page for SW-187 Himalayan Salt.

The current page describes it as:

  • cone 6 soft matte with light pink and burnt orange mottling
  • a glaze that pools glossy clear at cone 6
  • very mobile on vertical work, with Mayco recommending receding coats to avoid runoff
  • not recommended for dinnerware because of surface durability

Mayco also notes that wood and soda firing results vary, which is useful context when comparing this glaze to actual atmospheric salt or soda surfaces.
