Antonio Martinez presents this as a glaze he normally fires to cone 6 in oxidation, though he also notes it has behaved reliably for him at cone 10 reduction.

The key salt/soda-look detail is procedural rather than compositional:

  • dip the piece in Bates Clear
  • sprinkle soda ash onto selected wet areas
  • use the sodium addition to trigger localized runs and more varied surface movement

The page also exposes a full base recipe and an 8% Mason Stain 6376 Robin's Egg addition shown alongside the base glaze.
