Minori Thorpe describes spraying glazed pots with a solution made from equal weights of soda ash and baking soda before cone 6 electric firing.

The post matters because it isolates sodium surface treatment from the glaze body itself:

  • same glazed forms are shown with and without the soda spray
  • the sprayed examples read noticeably more atmospheric and disrupted
  • Thorpe reports no significant harmful effects on the kiln bricks and heating elements in that test

This is not a full glaze recipe source, but it is a strong process note for the salt/soda DIY track.
