What To Test Next

Open questions and structured comparison grids.

Open Research Actions

high priority

Build a commercial comparison grid

Create a tighter comparison grid for AMACO Potters Choice and Shino, Mayco Maycoshino and Micro Ash, and Coyote Archies Base, with attention to thickness, layering, and slow-cool behavior.

high priority

Build a salt-look electric test grid

Run a comparison set that separates sodium-driven DIY effects from commercial salt-track products: - Bates Clear with light and heavy soda-ash sprinkle - Visual Arts Center Shino at one and two dips, plus an iron-sprayed branch - A neutral glaze plus Minori-style soda spray - Mayco Sea Salt straight and over SW-106 Alabaster - Mayco Himalayan Salt on vertical texture - Decide whether Laguna WC-559 should live in the main electric program or in a separate cone 10 branch

medium priority

Define image and process capture standards

Document a consistent approach for image naming, image attribution, application thickness notes, clay body context, and firing schedule normalization.
