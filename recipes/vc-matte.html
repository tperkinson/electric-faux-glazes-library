V.C. Matte

A stable cone 6 matte base that becomes much more atmospheric when used underneath fake ash and ash-overlay recipes.

Cone 6 base for ash-look overlays Researched

Process

Application: Apply as the base layer underneath Hannahs Fake Ash or other runny atmospheric overlays.

Notes: The published combination image shows V.C. Matte under Hannahs Fake Ash, Ashleighs Rivulet, and Frasca Ash. Hall-Hough used shells and wadding under runny combinations.

Fit: By itself it is matte and non-running, but overlay combinations can run heavily.

Notes

V.C. Matte is important even though it is not the dramatic surface by itself.

  • It functions as the quiet stage that lets atmospheric overlays read clearly.
  • This should be tested under both fake-ash and real wood-ash overlays because base glaze character often determines whether the final stack feels muddy or alive.
