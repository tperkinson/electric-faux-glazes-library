Notes

Tomato Red Base is the more constructed reduction-red recipe in this pass.

  • The Crocus Martis addition makes it especially interesting for testing depth and mineral complexity against simpler iron-red formulas.
  • It is a strong candidate for a side-by-side with commercial oxblood products because both are chasing the same visual family by different means.
