AMACO PC-70 Copper Red

A Potters Choice copper red that promises traditional reduction-copper results in oxidation, breaking clear where thin.

AMACO Potters Choice Cone 5-6

Effect

Finish tags: oxblood, glossy, breaks-clear

Effect tags: reduction-look, copper red

Atmosphere: oxidation

Use Notes

Application: AMACO says Copper Red gives the same results in oxidation as a traditional reduction copper glaze. Apply 3 coats for best results and do not thin the glaze. The official page also shows layered vase examples using PC-70 with Potter's Choice Flux glazes.

Food safe: Not specified

Sources: amaco-pc-70-copper-red, amaco-potters-choice

Notes

PC-70 is the cleaner AMACO entry point when the goal is specifically reduction-red language rather than the broader Potters Choice family.

  • It belongs in the same commercial test row as Coyote Oxblood so thickness and color quality can be compared directly.
  • The clear breaking at thin spots makes it especially useful on edges and carved transitions.
  • The official gallery now included here also shows how PC-70 behaves in flux-layered combinations, not only as a flat chip.
