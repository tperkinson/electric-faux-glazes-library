Mayco SW218 Micro Ash

A fluid commercial glaze that can read like a modern ash surface, with charcoal depth, periwinkle float, and micro-crystalline bloom.

Mayco Stoneware Cone 6

Effect

Finish tags: glossy, translucent, micro-crystalline, charcoal

Effect tags: periwinkle float, onyx bloom, texture break

Atmosphere: oxidation or reduction

Use Notes

Application: Mayco recommends cone 6 oxidation for crystalline development. The product is fluid at three coats, so application should recede near the base to avoid runoff.

Food safe: Not specified

Sources: mayco-micro-ash, mayco-glaze-combinations

Notes

Micro Ash is useful because it sits between ash-look and crystalline behavior.

  • At cone 6 it promises the most complex surface in the first commercial batch.
  • It should be tested at two and three coats, and on texture, because both movement and crystal development are thickness-dependent.
