Mayco SW-187 Himalayan Salt

A soft matte glaze with light pink and burnt-orange mottling that pools glossy clear at cone 6, giving a flashed salt- or soda-adjacent read when used with enough texture and thickness contrast.

Mayco Stoneware Matte Cone 6-10

Effect

Finish tags: soft matte, mottled, gloss-pooling

Effect tags: salt-look, pink-orange mottling, mobile, breaks on texture

Atmosphere: oxidation

Use Notes

Application: Mayco warns that the glaze can be very mobile, recommends receding coats on vertical work, and does not recommend it for dinnerware because of surface durability.

Food safe: Not specified

Sources: mayco-himalayan-salt

Notes

This is likely the easier commercial route toward a warm flashed salt/soda color story.

  • It should be tested on textured forms where the gray break and clear pooling can separate visually.
  • Because mobility is part of the look, application range matters more than with a stable satin matte.
