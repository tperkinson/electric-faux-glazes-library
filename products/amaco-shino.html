AMACO Shino

A commercial Shino line aimed at heavy iron reduction looks in oxidation, suitable for red or white clay and layerable with Potters Choice.

AMACO Shino Cone 5-6

Effect

Finish tags: iron, gloss-to-matte, shino-like

Effect tags: reduction-look, traditional-japanese, layerable

Atmosphere: oxidation

Use Notes

Application: AMACO describes this line as blendable for gloss or matte variation and suitable for cone 5-6. It can also be layered with the Potters Choice line for broader effects.

Food safe: Yes

Sources: amaco-shino-line, amaco-potters-choice

Notes

This is likely the cleanest off-the-shelf route toward shino-ish electric results without mixing a raw formula.

  • It should be tested on both white and red stoneware because the official description calls out both.
  • It belongs in the same comparison grid as Maycoshino because both target iron-rich, traditional surface language.
